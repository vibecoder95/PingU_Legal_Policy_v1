Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use PallyMatch, we collect:

  • Email address for account authentication
  • Profile information (age, gender, preferences)
  • Chat messages and timestamps
  • Device information and usage data
  • Reports and moderation actions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide anonymous matching services
  • Ensure user safety and moderation
  • Improve our service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary for moderation and safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • To protect rights and safety of users

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data (available in app settings)
  • Delete your account and data
  • Request data correction
  • Withdraw consent at any time

5.1 Data Deletion Rights

Deleting a Chat from Your List:

  • When you delete a chat from your list, it is removed from your view only
  • The chat and messages remain on our servers if other participants still have access
  • Other users in the chat can continue to see the conversation
  • Chats are permanently deleted from servers only when all participants delete them, or when you delete your account

Deleting Your Account:

  • When you delete your account, all your messages are permanently deleted from our servers immediately
  • All chat documents where you participated are deleted from our servers
  • Your account information, profile data, and all associated data are removed from our servers
  • Account deletion is immediate, permanent, and irreversible
  • For complete details, see our Account Deletion Policy

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use Firebase Analytics for service improvement. You can control tracking through your device settings.
